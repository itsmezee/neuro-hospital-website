About Dr. GVKR

Dr. G. V. Krishna Reddy is a renowned neurologist with over 15 years of clinical experience. He completed his DM in Neurology from a prestigious institute and has been instrumental in establishing advanced neurological care in Mahabubnagar region.

He specializes in stroke management, epilepsy treatment, and movement disorders. Dr. GVKR has treated over 5000 neurological patients and is known for his patient-centric approach and clinical excellence.
