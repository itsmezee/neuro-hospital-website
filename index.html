Stroke Excellence

Stroke is a time-sensitive neurological emergency. Brain cells begin dying within minutes. Early intervention significantly improves recovery.

  • Thrombolysis effective within 4.5 hours
  • Rapid neurological evaluation is critical
  • ICU monitoring improves outcomes

Neurosurgery & Spine

Structured surgical care when clinically indicated for Brain (SDH/EDH/ICH), Spine (Disc prolapse, Myelopathy), and Interventional Pain Therapy.
