Frequently Asked Questions

What should I do in case of a neurological emergency?

Call our emergency number (094940 67108) immediately. For stroke symptoms (FAST: Face drooping, Arm weakness, Speech difficulty, Time to call), don't wait - every minute counts. Our team will guide you on immediate steps and prepare for your arrival.

Do I need a referral to see a neurologist?

No, you don't need a referral. You can book an appointment directly with our neurologists. However, if you have existing medical records or referrals from other doctors, please bring them to your consultation.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Please bring: 1) All previous medical records, 2) Recent test results and imaging reports, 3) List of current medications, 4) Insurance information if applicable, 5) A family member or friend if possible.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept most major insurance providers. Please contact our billing department at 094940 67108 or email billing@gvkrneurocare.com to verify your insurance coverage before your appointment.
