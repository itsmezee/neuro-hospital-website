Frequently Asked Questions

What should I do in case of a neurological emergency?

Call our emergency number (094940 67108) immediately. For stroke symptoms (FAST), don't wait - every minute counts.

Do I need a referral to see a neurologist?

No, you don't need a referral. You can book an appointment directly with our neurologists.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Please bring: 1) Previous medical records, 2) Recent test results, 3) List of current medications, 4) Insurance information.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept most major insurance providers. Please contact our billing department for verification.
