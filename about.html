About Dr. GVKR's Neuro Care

Pioneering neurological excellence in Mahabubnagar with compassion and cutting-edge care

Our Story & Mission

Founded by Dr. GVKR, our neuro care center has been at the forefront of neurological treatment in Mahabubnagar and surrounding regions for over a decade. What began as a humble clinic has grown into a comprehensive neurological care facility, serving thousands of patients with brain and nerve disorders.

Our mission is simple yet profound: to provide exceptional neurological care that combines medical excellence with compassionate patient-centered service. We believe that every patient deserves access to the best neurological treatments, regardless of their background or condition.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted neurological care center in Telangana, recognized for clinical excellence, innovative treatments, and unwavering commitment to patient well-being.
